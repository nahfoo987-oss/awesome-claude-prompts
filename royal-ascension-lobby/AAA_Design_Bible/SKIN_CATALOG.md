# Crown Chaos: Royal Ascension — Skin Catalog Design Bible

**Game:** Crown Chaos: Royal Ascension
**Version:** 1.0
**Visual Identity:** Dark Premium — Black Onyx · Gunmetal · Royal Gold · Violet Corruption · White Marble

---

## Overview

This document defines every cosmetic skin available at launch across four equipment slots. All skins are purely cosmetic and confer no gameplay advantage. The visual language is unified: corrupted royalty, fallen empires, void energy, and the weight of a crown no one deserves but everyone will kill to hold.

### Rarity Reference

| Label | Color Code | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Common | Grey `#9E9E9E` | Earnable through normal play |
| Rare | Blue `#4FC3F7` | Mid-tier grind or low-cost purchase |
| Epic | Purple `#CE93D8` | Meaningful effort or moderate Robux spend |
| Legendary | Gold `#FFD700` | Prestige tier, high cost or deep Battle Pass |
| Mythic | Red/Black `#B71C1C` / `#0D0D0D` | Ultra-rare; Robux or achievement-locked only |

### Unlock Method Reference

| Method | Description |
|---|---|
| Crown Coins | In-game currency earned by playing matches |
| Robux | Real-money Roblox currency |
| Battle Pass | Seasonal tier unlock (Tiers 1–50, one per season) |
| Founder / Achievement | Exclusive; earned by being in the first 100 players or completing a named challenge |

## Visual Style Notes for Art Team

**Palette constraints (strictly enforced):**
- Primary base colors: `#0D0D0D` (void black), `#2C2C2C` (gunmetal), `#F5F5F0` (white marble), `#FFD700` (royal gold)
- Accent glow: `#7B2FBE` (violet corruption), `#B71C1C` (mythic red)
- No saturated primaries. No pastels. No rainbow gradients.

**Material language:**
- Metals should look cold and heavy — no warm bronze unless tarnished
- Glow effects should feel like corruption or void energy, never celestial or angelic
- Marble should be cracked or aged — not pristine
- Legendary and Mythic items should have particle effects visible from across the arena

**Effect scale rule:**
- Common: static or near-static materials, minimal VFX
- Rare: subtle ambient effect (shimmer, low particle count)
- Epic: persistent VFX, visible glow, animated texture
- Legendary: full particle system, distinct visual identity at distance
- Mythic: full aura, unique animation loop, arena-presence-level visual weight
